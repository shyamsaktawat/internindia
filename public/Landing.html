Frequently asked questions

The internship program is structured over three months, with each month focusing on different aspects of learning and skill development.

The first month includes theoretical learning from industry experts, live interactive sessions, doubt-clearing sessions, and 4-5 weekly sessions via Zoom or Google Meet.

The second month focuses on applying concepts learned in the first month, working on real-time projects with mentor assistance, and completing both individual (7-day) and group (21-day) projects.

The third month includes multiple mock interview sessions, resume building assistance, and placement support to help prepare you for job opportunities.

The program provides internship certificates, competitive coding opportunities, community support, and access to a WhatsApp community for networking and additional resources.
